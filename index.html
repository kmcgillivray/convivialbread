Creating Conviviality

Conviviality is a friendly, lively atmosphere. The word comes from the Latin convivium, meaning “a feast”, which comes from con- “with” + vivere “live.”

An atmosphere of conviviality accompanied by local food is a key characteristic of both "blue zones", areas of the world with a high number of centenarians, and "third places", gathering places away from work and home that are essential to building strong, creative communities.
